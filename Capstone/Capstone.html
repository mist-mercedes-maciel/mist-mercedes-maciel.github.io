WELCOME

To the Tour Management II training.


   The Tour Management II training consist of five pages of learning materials that describe the process of opening and closing the Visitors Center, as well as the procedure to start and end the cash drawer, and one page with additional resources and instructions to sign up for an on-site VC demo tour.

   Thank you for agreeing to participate in the assessment project of the Tour Management II training. The purpose of this assessment is to evaluate the effectiveness of this training model, and to gather feedback.

As a participant you are ask to:

1. Respond to a twenty questions Pre-test and Survey

2. Complete this Tour Management II training

3. Respond to a twenty questions Post-test and feedback upon completion of the training.

   There are no passing or failing grade so don’t feel pressure by the term ‘Pre- or Post test’. Please be honest, your genuine answers will help us improve the overall training experience for current and future docents.

   Participants should expect to invest about 60 minutes completing this project.
