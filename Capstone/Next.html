Trainee Sign Up

After completing the Tour Management II learning website, your next step in the Visitors Center taining is to contact Todd, he will let you know the available dates for an on-site training. Once you have determined which date works for you, you will log-in to the volunteer's page in the Point Sur website, and sign up as a trainee for the tour.

Signing up as a Trainee will serve one of two purposes:

  • It will provide an on-site/hands-on demonstration by one of our experienced VC docent. This will help you to put in practice all the processes and procedures you have learn.

  • If you feel confident, this could be your opportunity to demonstrate the knowledge aquired and get the 'VC' section of the Docents check off list done.
