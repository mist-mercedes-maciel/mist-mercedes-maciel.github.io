Hover over the images for information that will help you make a decision.










How to Cancel

  1. Put down the 'Tour Cancelled' sign on the gate.
  2. Call the Big Sur Ranger Station to let them know in case they get questions.
  3. If you are doing the morning tour, inform D1 from next tour.
  4. Call the Point Sur Facebook chairman to post tour cancellation.
  5. Stay about fifteen past the scheduled tour start time, and put the tour cancellation sign down.
  6. Notify Scheduler, Chairman and Vice Chairman of Volunteers know that you cancel the tour.
