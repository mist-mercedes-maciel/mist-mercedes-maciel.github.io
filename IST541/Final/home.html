D1 Tour Management Refresher for a Regular Tour




This instructional website is intended to be a refresher for current volunteers on the core logistics steps and recommendations for giving a regular tour. It includes several pages with information on the necessary steps to conduct a successful tour, and one additional page with information on when and how to cancel a tour.



Leraning Objectives; after reviewing this website, current CCLK Docent volunteers will be able to:

  1. Follow the established process to safely and effectively access the park and prepare for the tour.
  2. Recall key interpretative points of interest used as time markers and the estimated arrival time to those markers.
  3. Recite the 5Ts and other rules and recommendations when welcoming visitors at the Highway 1 gate.
  4. Recite the safety talk's main points before the interpretative tour begins and after the tour has ended.
  5. Follow the established process to cancel a tour.
