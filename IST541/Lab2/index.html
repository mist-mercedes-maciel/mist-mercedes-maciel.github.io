How to Make Pumpkin Spice Syrup

By Mercedes Maciel


Step 1: Gather the Ingredients and Utensils

Tis the season for some pumpkin everything!  If you enjoy the most sought after seasonal flavor at Starbucks but hate the price tag, here is a great recipe for a pumpkin spice syrup that will become an autumn staple in your kitchen.

  You will need the following ingredients and kitchen utensils:

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 cup of water
  • 8 tbs of pumpkin pie filling, devided
  • 4 cinnamon sticks
  • 4 tsp of grated nutmeg
  • 2 tsp of fresh ginger
  • 2 tsp of whole cloves
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
  • 1/4 cup of sweetened condensed milk

Utensils

  • 1 quart suacepan
  • Measuring cup
  • Measuring spoons
  • Small rubber spatula
  • Strainer
  • Mason jar

Step 2: Make Simple Syrup
  • Mix water and sugar in the saucepan: bring to a solf boil
  • Redice the heat to medium-low and stir regularly until the sugar is completely dissolved (about 5 minutes)

tips_and_updates This is a typical simple syrup it can be use as is or be infused with herbs or spices such as lavender, mint, cinnamon to sweetened soft beverages, cocktails, and pastries.


Step 3: Add Spices
  • Stir 6 tablespoons of pumpkin filling, cinnamon sticks, nutmeg, ginger, cloves, vanilla, and pumpkin pie spice
  • Bring to a simmer and cook until mixture is well combined (about 8 minutes)

Step 4: String Mixture
  • Carefully, strain mixture into a bowl and discard any solids
  • Once strain, return the liquid to the saucepan over low heat

tips_and_updates If you don't have a small strainer, you can use cheesecloth or even coffee filters.


Step 5: Add the Rest of the Ingredients
  • Stir condensed milk and the remaining 2 tablespoons of pumpkin filling into the syrup
  • Cook until dissolved stirring regularly (about 5 minutes)
  • Once combined, let cool down and transfer to mason jar and store up to two weeks in the fridge

Bonus Step: Enjoy! 

You can enjoy this recipe on cold or hot beverages, just like at your favorite Starbucks. Here is how to make a pumpkin spice latte.


  • Mix 3 tablespoons of pumpkin spice syrup
  • 1 to 2 shots of brewed espresso
  • 3/4 to 1 cup of steamed milk
  • Sprinkle cinnamon on top (optional)

Relax and Enjoy!
